# Human_3D_Papers

Assorted papers on pose-related human generation tasks, including 2D and 3D cases. Free at your disposal:)

Papers are categorized by topics and ordered by dates.

Feel free to send me PRs for updates. Also, you are welcome to leave a comment and discuss about a certain paper with me.
